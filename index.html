How I work

Designing the rhythm behind better teamwork

To me, being a PM isn't just about shipping products. I bring design thinking into how a team works together — the collaboration rhythm, the way we communicate through a process, the tools we share.

That means taking time to understand how teammates actually work, and finding a pace that's both efficient and comfortable. Without any budget or IT support, I built a Notion task board from scratch for my company — designing the view around people rather than projects, structuring the database logic, and driving adoption across the whole team. It quietly changed how we collaborate every day.
